Project Objective      
Cafe management is not only about making dishes and beverages for patrons; it involves creating menus, processing orders, tracking customer preferences, and assessing sales. Many small to mid-sized cafes are still performing these processes either manually or with simple tools. In either case, performing these tasks improperly or slowly can lead to customer dissatisfaction and missed sales opportunities. For this reason, it is imperative that we create a simple-to-use yet 'intelligent system' that will assist cafe owners in completing the tasks necessary for effective operation of the cafe. By streamlining the daily operations of the cafe and providing valuable insights through analytics, cafe managers will be able to improve their decision-making and their customers' experiences.      

Scope of the Project                                                                                                                                                        
This project focuses on building a console-based Smart Cafe Management System using Python. It covers essential cafe operations such as menu handling, order processing, and basic sales analysis. The system is designed to simulate real-world functionality in a simplified environment, making it suitable for learning and demonstration purposes. While it does not include advanced integrations like online payments or real-time databases, it lays a strong foundation that can be expanded in the future.
                                                                                                                                                                          
Target Users                                              
Cafe owners or small business operators looking for a structured way to manage their operations
Staff members who handle orders and billing
Students and beginners who want to understand how real-world management systems are designed and implemented

High-Level Features                                                                                                                                                            
Easy menu management with options to add, update, view, and delete items
Order processing system with input validation and receipt generation
Basic recommendation system that suggests popular or preferred items
Customer classification based on ordering patterns (VIP, Regular, etc.)
Sales simulation and simple performance visualization using text-based dashboards
Organized and modular code structure following good programming practices
